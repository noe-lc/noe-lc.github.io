Description

This animated map shows polygons of each physical retail location in the Manhattan borough, in New York City, where each day is compressed into a 10-second timespan. Each polygon takes on the value of the retail location which: a) is within the polygon's bounds, and b) is open the most time per week compared to others sharing the same polygon. The delay and duration of transitions for each polygon depend on their opening and closing hours.
